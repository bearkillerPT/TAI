ptu	Illaam indo dipalosaiannä', indo daham anna to ussäkei sangngim ma'baju bassi malea susi lelo api, malibulum, anna mahihhim. Indo daham sangngim ma'ulu singa anna illaam mai sädäna messubum api,
ptu	Anna indo olo'-olo' to peande to puham kende' sapo' pa'dem temo, la ia siam tomahaja kakahuanna. Anna ia siam mesa indo pitu tomahaja anna la nahua katilakaam lambi' mate sule lako salako-lakona.
ptu	Ia la umpassiianni uwai matanna. Anna la pa'dem pole' kamateam, tä'um deem pa'bahataam, tä'um deem to la sumahho, anna la pa'dem kamapi'disam. Aka ingganna-ingganna sia yolona pa'de asammi.”
ptu	Anna mane ma'kada Puang Yesus naua: “Kannassai, tä'um la masäe anna sulemä' sule! Napohongko' to untuhu'i ingganna kaha-kaha dipa'palosaam illaam inde suha'.”
